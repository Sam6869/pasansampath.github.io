About me

Background & Story

A quick overview of who I am, what I do, and what I care about in software quality.

I'm a results-driven QA professional based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, with over 8 years of hands-on experience across test automation, team leadership, and quality delivery in Agile environments.

I specialise in building Playwright and TypeScript automation frameworks integrated with CI/CD pipelines via GitHub Actions. I'm equally comfortable with API testing, performance benchmarking with Grafana k6, and mobile automation using Appium.

Beyond the technical work, I'm passionate about mentoring junior engineers and fostering a quality-first culture. I regularly run knowledge transfer sessions and believe great QA is a mindset — not just a role.

Currently leading a current project QA team at Calcey, delivering an EventTech platform used globally for conference and event management.

I am particularly interested in how quality engineering can move beyond defect detection into enabling faster, more reliable product delivery. My work often involves aligning testing efforts with business priorities, ensuring that what gets released is not only functional but meaningful to end users.

Passionate about continuous learning, test automation improvements, and evolving into a QA Lead role where I can contribute to building high-performing QA teams and delivering exceptional product quality.
